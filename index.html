Tennis Stats

Welcome to our visualisations. Choose a panel and click 'Choose Visualisation' on this screen to get started.

Pick a visualisation from that list to see it in the panel. Hover over entries to see more information (on some visualisations).

Click on the Player view tab to see more player-specific info.

Visualisations on the Player view tab will be based on the player chosen from the left

Click on a data-point in those visualisations to make that entry's player to appear in the right dropdown.

Click on the double-headed arrow button to switch round the active player with the player on the right.


  Enjoy!
